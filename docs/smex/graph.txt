GATING SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 • Iron at all          : blast furnace MELTING ⇒ requires hot blast ≥800°C (cowper loop)
 • Mechanical power (↯)  : blower (≥3 RPS) AND bessemer transmission
 • Blast gas            : intake → blower(Air→Blast) → cowper(heats) → consumer
 • Steel               : Bessemer needs Blast + MP + molten iron arriving >~1186°C
 • Keep metal hot       : long canals cool/solidify; route short or rely on bessemer reheat
 • Molds               : clayform plate/doubleingot/quadrod (200u, fired); small=pedestal,
                         large (anvil/helvehammer)=canal tap; molten mold spills unless held
